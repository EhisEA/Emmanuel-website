# How I Think

I see engineering primarily as **problem solving**.

Languages and frameworks change constantly.

But the ability to:

- understand systems
- reason through problems
- design architectures
- build reliable solutions

remains constant.

AI is now amplifying this process.

The most powerful shift I see is that:

> AI expands how quickly humans can move from idea → working system.

The future of engineering will likely involve **humans collaborating with AI systems to design, reason about, and build software.**

This is the frontier I am exploring.
